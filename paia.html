1. Introduction and Purpose

This manual is published in terms of Section 51 of the Promotion of Access to Information Act, 2 of 2000 (PAIA), which requires every private body to compile and make available a manual describing the records it holds and the process by which access to those records may be requested.

The purpose of PAIA is to give effect to the constitutional right of access to information held by both public and private bodies, to the extent that such access is required for the exercise or protection of any rights.

5. How to Request Access to Records

A request for access to records held by Corezality (Pty) Ltd must be made in writing using the prescribed Form C (available from the SAHRC website or on request from the Information Officer).

Requests must include:

  • The identity of the requester
  • A description of the records requested, with sufficient detail to identify them
  • The form in which access is requested (copy, inspection, etc.)
  • If the requester is seeking access on behalf of another person, proof of that capacity
  • The right being exercised or protected, and why access is required

Submit Form C to:
Information Officer, Corezality (Pty) Ltd t/a Artifacts Coffee
Email: artifacts.coffee.roastery@gmail.com

The Information Officer will respond within 30 days of receiving a complete request.

7. Grounds for Refusal

The Information Officer may refuse access to records on grounds permitted by PAIA, including but not limited to:

  • Protection of third-party personal information (POPIA)
  • Commercial confidentiality
  • Legal privilege
  • Records that could endanger life or safety
  • Records subject to ongoing legal proceedings

Any refusal will be communicated in writing with reasons, within the prescribed timeframe.
