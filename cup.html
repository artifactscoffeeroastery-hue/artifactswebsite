The Object

An Artefact
You Drink From

The name Artifacts Coffee came from a background in information systems — where an “artefact” is the deliberate, tangible output of a careful process. The cup follows that same logic.

Every dimension was considered. The weight in the hand. The matte finish that doesn’t mark. The cyan that doesn’t shout. This isn’t merchandise for the sake of it — it’s the vessel we wanted to drink our own coffee from, and we figured you might feel the same.

12oz because that’s the right size for a flat white or a long black without compromise. Matte ceramic because gloss belongs in commercial kitchens. Cyan because it’s ours.
